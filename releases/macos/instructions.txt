1) Download and unpack the zip file.
2) Turn OFF the diyPresso machine with the switch at the back. During updating the controller will be powered via the USB connection.
3) Get your USB cable ready, but do not yet connect it to your computer.
4) Double click "upload.command" in the directory where you unpacked the zip file.
5) Follow the instruction on screen. Do not press any button on the diyPresso machine during updating.
6) After the script is completed, you can disconnect the USB cable and power on your diyPresso machine.

Enjoy your coffee!
